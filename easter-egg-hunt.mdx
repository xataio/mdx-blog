As part of our launch week, for a chance to win one of five [Meta Quest 3 Headsets](https://www.meta.com/quest/quest-3/), we will be hiding an Easter egg **each day** across the Xata website or community channels for you to find, with a riddle to solve. There are five Easter eggs in total, with the egg of the day presented daily on our [launch week landing page](https://xata.io/launch-week-unleash-the-elephant).

![Find all five easter eggs for a chance to win!](/images/easter-egg-hunt-02.png)

**To enter:**

- Sign up to our community [Discord](https://xata.io/discord)
- Follow us on [X | Twitter](https://twitter.com/xata) and [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/xataio)
- Find the daily easter egg across our website and community channels to reveal the daily riddle
- Answer all 5 riddles and by submission you accept the [Terms & Conditions](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1rZ3bVB4BtiVyCHOVKWOhEJrpg0SWFGHFsQLzY0Eo0uI/)

**Closing date:** March 22 at 12pm Central European Time (CET).

**Prize drawing:** Winners will be announced live on the Xata Community Discord #Announcements channel on Wednesday, March 27 at 5pm Central European Time (CET).

**Daily hints:**
Stay tuned for daily hints on here, including our [launch page](https://xata.io/launch-week-unleash-the-elephant) and via our social channels.

- Day 1: Look for the pink Easter egg hiding somewhere on the Xata website
- Day 2: Look for the yellow Easter egg hiding somewhere on the Xata website
- Day 3: Look for the blue Easter egg...it's APParently not hidden on the website today
- Day 4: Look for the green egg in a place were we like to chat with our community and help them with all things Xata
- Day 5: Check back soon

Once you find the hidden egg, click to reveal the riddle to solve.
